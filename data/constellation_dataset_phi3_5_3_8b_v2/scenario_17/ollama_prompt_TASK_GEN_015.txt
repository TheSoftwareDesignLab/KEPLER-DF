As an Earth-observation mission operator, I am tasking a routine monitoring operation for multispectral vegetation analysis using near-infrared reflection capabilities. Our focus is on the archipelago of Kepulauan Meranti in Indonesia. This standard operational tasking is part of our baseline pipeline tracking and must be completed within four days, specifically by evening time at the latest. We aim to assess chlorophyll absorption levels or calculate a vegetation NDVI index, as well as surface reflectance characteristics. The data products are expected to be ingested into our system promptly following this deadline for continued environmental monitoring and analysis.
